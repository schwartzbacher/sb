featured work


Hannadé (Brand Logo Design)

Featured among our numerous works, is the brand identity design for Hannadé. Hannadé specializes in footwear and beaded-jewellery dealership. They craft and sell a large variety of locally made footwear and beads. The rationale behind this logo is the initials of the C.E.O. and Founder, Hannah Adejumo.



Minimalistic by Design

We believe that the logo is the ultimate elevator pitch to potential clients. it has to say a lot, yet look simple enough to remember. To this end, we have decided to use a minimalistic approach towards the project.

SCALABLE AND ADAPTABLE

This work was delivered in high standard raster and vector files, this effectively ensures that the logo can be printed in different sizes and across different media without losing it's visual appeal.

TIMELESS

Timelessness is a rather unpopular feature among the majority of logo design projects with some brands having to pass through a rather short re-design cycle. Trends come and go, but a timeless logo would stand this test of time. As you may see, we have put this into consideration while working with this brand.
